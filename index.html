About Us

Our company has a proud heritage of over 35 years. At one time, it stood as the backbone of the local community. In its early days, around 50 people were employed here, and many individuals from the New Bazaar area learned their skills with us before moving on to different paths. Over the years, we have been involved in the construction of thousands of homes and buildings. After the passing of Mohammed Haji, the company is now led by Hassan and Latheef, who continue to guide a dedicated team of 16 members
